Hypercosm is a Work in Progress mod that adds a variety of new sci-fi/tech and space related content. We have 5 dedicated developers who put all their passion into this mod!
As of right now, it currently contains:
2 new ores
1 new boss (Unfortunately, a worm boss)
71 new items
And a variety of new enemy npcs!

We have a discord server, which is useful for reporting bugs and suggesting new content. you can also go there just to relax and have fun.
